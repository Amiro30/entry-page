Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

This website allows users to leave optional tips. We respect your privacy and collect only the minimum data required to operate this service.

Data Controller

This website is operated by the site owner, who acts as the data controller for personal data collected through this service.

Information We Collect

When you leave a tip, we may collect:

  • Tip amount and currency
  • Optional nickname (if you choose to provide one)
  • Optional message attached to the tip
  • Date and time of the transaction
  • Referral source or campaign identifier (if provided)

Payments

All payments are processed securely by Stripe. We do not collect, store, or process your card details.

Stripe may collect personal and technical data in accordance with its own privacy policy:
https://stripe.com/privacy

Emails

Transactional emails related to optional tips may be sent using Brevo. Brevo processes data on our behalf in accordance with its own privacy and security standards.

How We Use Your Data

  • To process and record your optional tip
  • To display your nickname or message (if provided)
  • To prevent fraud and abuse
  • To understand traffic sources (e.g. YouTube, Instagram, TikTok)
  • To meet accounting and legal obligations

Legal Basis for Processing (UK/EU)

If you are located in the UK or EU, we process personal data based on:

  • Performance of a transaction (optional tip processing)
  • Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, analytics)
  • Legal obligations (accounting and compliance)

Data Retention

Tip records are retained only as long as necessary for accounting, legal, and security purposes. Optional personal data (nickname or message) may be deleted upon request.

Your Rights

If you are located in the UK or EU, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of optional personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing

Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, please contact:
support@andrewautohub.com
