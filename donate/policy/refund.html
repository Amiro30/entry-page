This website accepts voluntary donations only. Donations are made as a gesture of support and are not payments for goods, services, subscriptions, or digital products.

Refund Policy

Donations are generally non-refundable.

By completing a donation, you acknowledge and agree that:

  • The donation is voluntary
  • The donation amount is chosen by you
  • You are not purchasing a product or service
  • No goods, services, or benefits are provided in exchange

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Accidental donations
  • Incorrect donation amounts
  • Change of mind

Exceptions

Refunds may be provided only where required by applicable law, or in cases of confirmed fraud or technical error.

Submitting a refund request does not guarantee that a refund will be issued.
