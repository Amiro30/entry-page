Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

This website allows users to make voluntary donations. We respect your privacy and collect only the minimum data required to operate this service.

Information We Collect

When you make a donation, we may collect:

  • Donation amount and currency
  • Optional nickname (if you choose to provide one)
  • Optional message attached to the donation
  • Date and time of the transaction
  • Basic technical data (such as IP address and referral source) for security and analytics purposes

Payments

All payments are processed securely by Stripe. We do not collect, store, or process your card details.

Stripe may collect personal and technical data in accordance with its own privacy policy:
https://stripe.com/privacy

How We Use Your Data

  • To process and record your donation
  • To display your nickname or message (if provided)
  • To prevent fraud and abuse
  • To understand traffic sources (e.g. YouTube, Instagram, TikTok)

Data Retention

Donation records are stored for accounting, security, and legal purposes. You may request deletion of optional personal data (nickname or message) by contacting us.

Your Rights

If you are located in the UK or EU, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data.
